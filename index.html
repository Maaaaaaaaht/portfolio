Hello I'm Matthieu

I always had a passion for nature, languages, travel and anything related with craftmanship. When i was 15, i started my career as a florist back in France. I had the amazing opportunities to learn through an apprentiship as well as a 6 months training at renowned florist in Belgium.

In 2013, i made the decision to move to London in order to learn english and work as a florist. Moving here brought me so many opportunities. I got to work on some big scale events in the UK and Internationally.

In 2017 i went on a solo trip to Japan for a couple week. I had a fantastic experience but i wanted to see the country more in depth. So i organised myself to go back to spend a full year there.

In 2023, i want to challenge myself by learning how to code !

Why do i want to become a web developer

A while ago, my boss was telling me about the issues he had with the website of his flowers buisness. I already had an interest in UX and UI back then. Looking at his website i started wandering how everything was working in the background.

I continued to dig into it and slowly it came to my mind that maybe i could become a developer. We use it on a daily basis, but it never occured to me how it could work.

The more i researched, i can see the infinite possibilities that coding can create. I always liked creating, playing with colors and i think coding would be amazing tool to create. I'm looking forward to learn about it deeply and be able to collaborate on projects with other developpers.
